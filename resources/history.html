From 2017 to 1 Billion


Over 1 billion barcodes served!

BarcodeAPI.org originated in 2017 as an open-source SQA (Software Quality Assurance) tool designed for generating test barcodes. Its initial deployment within Amazon.com quickly propelled its popularity, attracting a diverse global user base. Over the past 7+ years, BarcodeAPI.org has evolved into a cornerstone for barcode generation, serving millions of barcodes monthly across various industries and applications.

In 2024, in response to growing demand and to sustain the service's development and operational needs, we introduced a new phase with paid subscription options. This strategic move ensures continued accessibility for users who generate a limited number of barcodes while offering a subscription model for those with high-volume needs. This model allows us to maintain our commitment to open-source principles while ensuring sustainability and ongoing innovation.

BarcodeAPI.org remains dedicated to being your premier online resource for all barcode needs. Whether you're testing software, managing inventory, or integrating barcodes into innovative applications, we are here to support your journey with reliable, scalable, and user-friendly barcode generation solutions.

Join us as we continue to shape the future of barcode technology together!
